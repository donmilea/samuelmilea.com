A Little Help

This was my score for the film, A Little Help. The film was produced by the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts as a showcase for the graduating actors, and was directed by Edward Hicks.

The music was scored for string orchestra and piano, and recorded at Air Edel Studios with musicians from the Royal Academy of Music.
