Vanishing Points

Written after intensive study of the music of Stravinsky, I wanted to write a piece that followed the block-like structure of his early pieces. Vanishing Points is made up of contrasting sections that are juxtaposed with one another. The sections are different in terms of their energy and mood, but they're all bound by a 6-note pitch-set, from which all of the harmonic and melodic material is derived.

Vanishing Points was commissioned by the Royal Academy of Music and was performed by the Academy Symphonic Winds, conducted by Keith Bragg on 8th February 2019.
