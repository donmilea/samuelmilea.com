composer. musician.

I've always loved the sound of old string instruments, especially when they have been made from the same materials that would have been used 500 years ago. It's inspiring to know that the sounds and timbres you hear from these authenic instruments are the same ones that would have been heard centuries ago.

Here's a piece that I composed for the bass viol, entitled Unvoiced. Performed by Liam Byrne at the Royal Academy of Music.

Here's a selection of cues from the film A Little Help, which was created in collaboration with the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts.
