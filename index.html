I'm a composer and musician based in Wiltshire, England.

The music that I write encompasses many styles and influences, and involves a variety of instruments. Recently, I've been composing a piece for the bass viol, intended as a 2nd movement to my piece from 2019, entitled Unvoiced. As well as this, I've been compiling a book of Tudor-inspired compositions for beginner and intermediate guitarists.

In 2019, I graduated with an MA in Composition from the Royal Academy of Music. Whilst there, I studied with David Sawer and Morgan Hayes. Prior to this, I studied for a BMus in Composition with Param Vir at the University of West London.

Over recent years, I've had the pleasure to compose for a variety of fantastic ensembles and soloists. These include the Hermes Experiment; the Ligeti Quartet; bass violist Liam Byrne; the London Metropolitan Brass; the Royal Academy of Music Orchestra; and soprano Pia Davila. Alongside this, I've worked on collaborative film projects with the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, as well as acted as Producer for singer-songwriters.

My music has been performed in venues in the UK and internationally, such as LSO St Luke's, London; the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts, London; The Vaults, London; Chapel Arts, Cheltenham; Kunst-Universität, Graz; Heidelberger Frühling, Germany; and the Royal Academy of Music, London. As a Producer, I've enjoyed radio play on BBC Radio Wiltshire and BBC Introducing.

In the past, I've received generous support from the RVW Trust, the Countess of Munster Musical Trust and the Leathersellers’ Company Charitable Trust.
