Samuel Milea is a composer based in Wiltshire, England

Unvoiced

I've always loved the sound of old string instruments, especially when they've been made from historically authenic materials. I find inspiration in the fact that the sounds and timbres produced by these instruments are the same ones that would have been heard centuries ago.

Here's a piece that I composed for the bass viol, entitled Unvoiced. Performed by Liam Byrne at the Royal Academy of Music.

Here's a selection of cues I composed for the short film, A Little Help. This was a collaborative project with the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts.

Where She Has Lain

I enjoy composing music for unusual instrument combinations. This is a piece composed for the Hermes Experiement, with a setting of the poem with an instrumentation of soprano, clarinet, harp, and double-bass.
